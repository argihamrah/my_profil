THIS IS ARGI

Argi Hamrah is an Iranian-German photographer living in Barcelona with her family
She was brought up in Germany and discovered.
Her affection for photography at an early age through her father a hobby video/photographer by using his tools in exploring the word behind the cam.
The fascination continued after traveling to US, where she started to study the basic of manual analog photography with black-and-white film development in UC Berkeley.
Direct after returning back home she continued her passion with the journey of attending 3 years of work at “Marco Stirn Photography” and education training in Wiesbaden in learning the inside out of analog photography and later digital photography.
Since then we have been a freelancer in portrait, wedding and street photography.
